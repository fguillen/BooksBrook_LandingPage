Books Brook - Berlin

join Books Brook and you can buy one award winner graphic novel once and exchange it for another award winner graphic novel when you want and as many times you want for 6€ each exchange.

How it works and how much it total costs

#1 Buy your award winner graphic novels

for 20€ each graphic novel you earn the right to exchange it as many times you want and when you want

#2 Receive your graphic novels

you receive at your home as many graphic novels as you bought. This costs 4€ per shipment and 6€ per each graphic novel in the package

#3 Exchange your graphic novels for other ones

whenever you decide to exchange your already read graphic novel you can put them in the post, free of charge. And you go back to step #2.

Questions and answers

if you don't find your answers here you can contact us to make your questions.

Ok, how much does it really cost?

20€ for first purchase of a graphic novel. 4€ for each shipment. 6€ por each graphic novel exchanged.

Still confuse, give me an example of how much I will pay

if you decide to purchase 2 novels in our service, the cost will be: 1st delivery: 56€, 2nd delivery and following: 16€ each.

Can I choose the graphic novels I receive?

yes. we will contact you to see what of our graphic novels you are interested on. We can not warranty in which delivery will be each graphic novel but we will be sure we select only the ones you have chosen.

When do I have to return the graphic novels?

whenever you want. You can even keep them if you want. Just be aware that after 6 months of not exchange a graphic novel it be removed from our inventory and you need to make a 1st purchase again to recover the number of graphic novels in your exchanges.

Can I keep a graphic novel if I like it so much?

yes, see above.

Will I receive only brand new graphic novels?

nop, the graphics novels are co-owned for all members. We ask the members to treat the graphic novels with care. We will try to remove from our inventory any graphic novel that is not in good conditions.

In which language are the graphic novels?

english, all english.

Why all in english?

because we are small, and we want to remain small, and we have not capacity to keep inventories in different languages.
